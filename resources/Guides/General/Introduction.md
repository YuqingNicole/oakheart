# Oakheartglobal 桌游制造 - 公司介绍

**关于我们 | 精密制造传承 | 全链服务升级 | 全球化视野**

Oakheartglobal Manufacturing 是一家专注于桌游全产业链服务的制造企业。我们以精密塑料公仔制造起家，现已发展为提供从概念设计到成品交付的一站式桌游制造解决方案供应商，服务于全球独立设计师、出版商和品牌客户。

Oakheartglobal 成立于 2016 年，最初专注于高精度塑料微缩模型和游戏公仔的研发与生产。凭借在注塑成型、模具开发和精密制造领域的深厚积累，我们为众多知名桌游品牌提供了核心配件制造服务。经过多年的技术沉淀与市场洞察，我们于近年完成战略转型，整合了印刷、木制品、金属加工、组装包装等全产业链资源，成为能够独立承接桌游项目全流程生产的综合性制造商。我们的生产基地位于中国制造业核心区域，拥有完善的供应链网络和质量管理体系，确保每一款产品都能达到国际一流标准。

我们的客户涵盖从获奖出版商到独立游戏设计师的广泛群体。无论是简单的卡牌游戏，还是包含复杂微缩模型的大型战棋，Oakheartglobal 都能将客户的创意转化为现实。客户选择我们，是因为 Oakheartglobal 专注于高质量生产、卓越的客户服务，以及对桌游行业的深度参与。我们在塑料公仔制造领域积累的技术优势，使我们能够挑战行业极限，推动桌游配件工艺的持续创新。

Oakheartglobal 团队的每一位成员都是桌游爱好者，我们致力于推动桌游文化的发展与传播。我们定期参加国际桌游展会和行业活动，与全球合作伙伴保持紧密联系，及时掌握行业趋势——当然，也会抽时间玩游戏！我们对众筹项目有着丰富的经验，熟悉其独特的挑战与机遇。翱翔承诺提供无与伦比的客户服务，我们的团队不仅精通中英文，更懂得“桌游语言”。我们的热情在于制造高品质游戏，并在这一过程中建立有意义的合作关系。这也是翱翔的使命宣言：“制造（并畅玩！）业界最优秀的桌游产品。”

# About Our Company (English Version)

**Precision Manufacturing Heritage | Full-Chain Service Evolution | Global Perspective**

Oakheartglobal Manufacturing is a comprehensive board game manufacturing company specializing in end-to-end tabletop game production services. Starting from precision plastic miniature manufacturing, we have evolved into a one-stop solution provider offering services from concept design to finished product delivery, serving independent designers, publishers, and brand clients worldwide.

Founded in 2016, Oakheartglobal initially focused on high-precision plastic miniatures and game figurine development and production. With deep expertise in injection molding, mold development, and precision manufacturing, we have provided core component manufacturing services for numerous renowned board game brands. After years of technical accumulation and market insight, we completed a strategic transformation, integrating printing, woodworking, metal fabrication, assembly, and packaging resources across the entire supply chain. We have become a comprehensive manufacturer capable of independently handling full-cycle board game production. Our manufacturing facilities are located in China's core manufacturing region, with a complete supply chain network and quality management system ensuring every product meets world-class standards.

Our clients range from award-winning publishers to independent game designers. Whether it's a simple card game or a large-scale wargame with complex miniatures, Oakheartglobal turns our clients' concepts into reality. Customers choose us because Oakheartglobal focuses on high-quality production, excellent customer service, and deep engagement with the board game industry. Our technical advantages accumulated in plastic miniature manufacturing enable us to challenge industry limits and drive continuous innovation in board game component craftsmanship.
